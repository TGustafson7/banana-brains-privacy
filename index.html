Privacy Policy

Banana Brains — Math for Little Brains
Banana Brains is designed for children ages 4–6. We take privacy seriously — especially for young users. This policy explains exactly what data we collect (very little) and how we use it (we don't).

What Data We Collect

Banana Brains does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app works entirely on your device.

The only data stored is your child's game progress — current level, banana count, best streak, and unlocked stickers. This is saved locally on your device using standard browser storage (localStorage). It never leaves your device and is never sent to any server.

No Account Required

Banana Brains does not require an account, login, email address, or any personal information to play. There is no registration of any kind.

No Third-Party Services

Banana Brains does not use any third-party analytics, advertising networks, tracking services, or social media integrations. There are no ads in the app.

No Internet Connection Required

The app works fully offline. No data is transmitted over the internet at any time during normal use.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Banana Brains is designed for children under 13. We comply fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or anyone else.

Device Permissions

Banana Brains may request access to your device's text-to-speech system to read questions aloud (Voice feature). This is optional and can be turned off in the app settings. No audio is recorded or transmitted.

Data Deletion

All local game progress can be deleted at any time by using the "Start Over from Level 1" option in the app's settings screen, or by clearing your browser/app data through your device settings.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we will post the updated version at this URL with a new effective date. We do not anticipate changes as the app does not collect data.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the app, please contact us at: hello@bananabrainsapp.com
